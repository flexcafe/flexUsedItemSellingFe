## How to Request Deletion
If you want to delete your Flex Used Market account and related data:

1. Open the request link above.
2. Send an email from the address linked to your account, if possible.
3. Include your registered phone number, email address, and nickname so we can locate your account.
4. State clearly that you want your Flex Used Market account deleted.
5. If you cannot access the original email address, include enough details for us to verify your identity.
6. After verification, we will process your request and confirm once deletion is completed.

## Data Deleted
When we receive a verified deletion request, we will delete or anonymize account-related data tied to your profile, including:

- Name or nickname
- Phone number
- Email address
- Password and login credentials
- Profile photo or avatar
- Gender, age, marital status, and region
- GPS location data collected for registration or location-based features
- KBZ Pay name and phone number
- Facebook linking data such as Facebook name, profile URL, Facebook ID, and link status
- Product listings and listing images
- Chat messages and direct-trade content, where allowed by law and system limitations
- Reports, reviews, submissions, and verification files you uploaded
- Notification history and account activity associated with your profile

## Data Retained
We may keep certain information after deletion when needed for legitimate business, legal, or security purposes. This may include:

- Transaction or payment records
- Fraud prevention and abuse-prevention records
- Security logs and system audit logs
- Records required for accounting, compliance, or dispute resolution

## Retention Periods
Our standard retention periods are:

- Account profile data: deleted within 30 days after the request is verified
- Backup copies: removed within 90 days after deletion is completed
- Required legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention records: retained only as long as necessary and then deleted or anonymized

If a longer retention period is required by law or needed to resolve a dispute, protect against fraud, or enforce our terms, we may retain the relevant data for that purpose only.

## Notes
- Deleting the account may not immediately remove content that was already shared with other users, such as messages, reviews, or marketplace interactions.
- Some technical or security logs may remain in backups for a limited period before automatic deletion.
- Once deletion is completed, account access will be disabled.
