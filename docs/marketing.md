# Flex Used Market

Flex Used Market is a marketplace app for buying and selling used items with ease. Browse listings, post items, chat with other users, and manage your marketplace activity in one place.

## What You Can Do
- Create and manage your account
- Post items for sale
- Browse marketplace listings
- Chat with buyers and sellers
- Use profile and verification features
- Receive notifications and account updates

## Why Use Flex Used Market
Flex Used Market helps make local trading simple, fast, and convenient. It brings together the core tools you need for a better used-item marketplace experience on mobile.
