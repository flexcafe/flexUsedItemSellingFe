# Flex Used Market Support

If you need help with Flex Used Market, please contact us using the information below.

## Support Email
**Email:** `flexcafe223@hotmail.com`

## What We Can Help With
We can help with:
- Account login or registration issues
- Facebook linking or verification problems
- Profile and listing issues
- Chat or marketplace feature problems
- Account deletion requests
- General app feedback and bug reports

## What to Include in Your Message
To help us respond faster, please include:
- Your account email or phone number
- A short description of the issue
- Screenshots if available
- Your device model and operating system version

## Response Time
We will try to respond as soon as possible. If your issue needs account verification or investigation, it may take longer.

## Important Note
If your message is about deleting your account or data, please use the deletion request instructions provided in the app or the account deletion page.
