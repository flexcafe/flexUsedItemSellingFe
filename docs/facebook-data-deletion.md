# Facebook Data Deletion Instructions

**App Name:** Flex Used Market  
**Developer / Support:** Flex Used Market  
**Support Email:** flexcafe223@hotmail.com

This page explains how a user can request deletion of personal data connected to their Flex Used Market account and any Facebook-linked information.

## How to Request Deletion
If you want us to delete your account data, please send an email to:

`flexcafe223@hotmail.com`

Include the following information if possible:
- Your registered phone number
- Your email address
- Your nickname or display name
- A clear message saying that you want your Flex Used Market account deleted
- Any Facebook-linked account details that can help us identify your profile, such as your Facebook name or profile URL

If you cannot access the original email address, send enough information for us to verify that you own the account.

## What We Delete
After we verify your request, we will delete or anonymize account-related data that is tied to your profile, including:
- Name or nickname
- Phone number
- Email address
- Password and login credentials
- Profile photo or avatar
- Gender, age, marital status, and region
- Location data collected for app features
- Facebook linking data such as Facebook name, profile URL, Facebook ID, and link status
- Product listings and listing images
- Chat messages and direct-trade content, where allowed by law and system limitations
- Reports, reviews, submissions, and verification files you uploaded
- Notification history and account activity associated with your profile

## What We May Keep
Some information may be retained for legitimate business, legal, or security purposes, such as:
- Transaction or payment records
- Fraud prevention and abuse-prevention records
- Security logs and system audit logs
- Records required for accounting, compliance, or dispute resolution

## Retention Time
Our usual retention timeline is:
- Account profile data: deleted within 30 days after the request is verified
- Backup copies: removed within 90 days after deletion is completed
- Required legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention records: kept only as long as necessary and then deleted or anonymized

If we must keep certain information longer because of legal obligations, fraud prevention, or dispute handling, we will only retain the minimum data needed for that purpose.

## Important Notes
- Deleting your account may not immediately remove content that was already shared with other users, such as messages, reviews, or marketplace interactions.
- Some technical or security logs may remain in backups for a limited time before automatic deletion.
- Once deletion is completed, account access will be disabled.
